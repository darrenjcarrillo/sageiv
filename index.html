THE XEO® LASER DOES IT ALL

Our XEO® laser system uses light-based energy to address a range of aesthetic concerns non-invasively and with little-to-no downtime after treatment.

Get the smooth, hairless skin you want without irritation and razor burn. XEO® is exceptional at reducing hair on your arms, legs, back, and chest. No downtime is required, and results last for several months after your treatment course.

LIVE ACNE-FREE

AviClear™ is the first and only FDA-cleared energy device for treating mild, moderate, and severe acne. The device’s advanced technology allows it to treat all skin types, regardless of pigment. Most importantly, it can perform this action without added medication or procedures. Treatments take only 30 minutes, and the results are spotless!
